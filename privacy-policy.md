# Privacy Policy for TravelAssist

**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026

Thank you for using **TravelAssist**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Location Data

TravelAssist may access your device’s location to provide core features such as:

* Route planning and navigation
* Destination tracking and alerts
* Map-based trip visualization

Your location data is used only while using the app and is **not stored on our servers**.

---

### b. User Content (GPX Files)

TravelAssist allows you to import, create, and manage GPX (GPS Exchange Format) files.

* These files are stored **locally on your device** or in your personal iCloud storage (if enabled).
* We do **not access, upload, or share your GPX data externally**.

---

### c. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

* Location access (for navigation features)
* File access (for importing/exporting GPX files)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

* Provide navigation and trip planning features
* Display routes and maps
* Enable alerts based on your journey

We do **not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes**.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not sell, trade, or share** your personal data with third parties.

Your data remains:

* On your device
* Within Apple’s secure ecosystem (e.g., iCloud, if enabled)

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

TravelAssist uses Apple-provided frameworks such as:

* Apple MapKit (for maps and navigation)
* Apple iCloud (optional, for file synchronization)

These services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies.

---

## 5. Data Security

We prioritize your data security:

* No personal data is stored on external servers
* All processing happens locally on your device

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

TravelAssist does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

---

## 7. Your Choices

You can:

* Disable location access anytime in your device settings
* Delete GPX files stored on your device
* Disable iCloud sync if you prefer not to store files in the cloud

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a revised "Effective Date".
