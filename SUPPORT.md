# TravelAssist – Support

## Overview
TravelAssist helps you plan and monitor your journeys so you always know what’s next, what’s nearby, and when to move.

---

## Features

### 🗺️ Plan Your Journey
- Create a Journey Plan with multiple stops (date & time)
- Select your travel mode and configure lead time
- Preview routes for all planned stops  
- Optional round trip routing back to your current location

### 🚦 Monitor Trips in Real-Time
- Start monitoring to track your journey progress
- Receive time-sensitive alerts based on ETA or distance
- Get attention through a discreet “fake call” style alert

### 📊 At-a-Glance Updates
- Live Activity & Widgets for quick access
- Real-time status updates while traveling

### 🕘 History & Sync
- View past trips and monitoring activity
- Optional iCloud sync across devices

---

## Privacy
TravelAssist uses your location to:
- Calculate routes
- Estimate arrival times (ETA)
- Trigger arrival alerts

Your data:
- Stays on your device by default
- Is only synced via iCloud if you enable it

---

## Troubleshooting

### Location not updating?
- Ensure Location Services are enabled
- Set permission to “Always” for best results

### Not receiving alerts?
- Check Notifications are enabled
- Verify Background App Refresh is ON

### Live Activity not visible?
- Ensure Live Activities are enabled in system settings
